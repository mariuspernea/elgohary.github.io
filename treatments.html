Conditions

Mr Elgohary has a specialist interest and deals with the following conditions:

  • Complex and complicated cataract surgery e.g. traumatic, uveitis and diabetic cataract
  • Retinal tears and detachment repair
  • Macular hole repair
  • Treatment of diabetic retinopathy and its complications
  • Repair of ocular trauma and complications
  • Age-related macular degeneration

Procedures

Mr Elgohary performs the following procedures

  • Complex and complicated cataract surgery e.g. traumatic, uveitis and diabetic cataract
  • Retinal tears and detachment repair

Research

Mr Elgohary has research interests covering the treatment of submacular haemorrhage in age-related macular degeneration, lens implants in aphasic patients and risk factors for recurrence of retinal detachment. His work and publications on these topics can be found here:

https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=Elgohary%20M%20AND%20Eye&sort=date

Awards and Memberships

Mr Elgohary has received two Clinical Excellence Awards for setting up and developing the Vitreoretinal Service at Kingston Hospital. His professional memberships include:

  • British & Eire Vitreoretinal Society (BEAVRS)
  • European Society of Retinal Specialists (Euretina)
  • European Vitreoretinal Society (EVRS)
  • American Academy of Ophthalmology (AAO)
